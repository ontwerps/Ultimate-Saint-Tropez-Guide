# Beaches

## Summary

This chapter helps readers choose between easy beaches, quieter coves, snorkelling spots, beaches with services and places that work best early or late in the day.

## Criteria

Access, parking, water quality, snorkelling, services, crowds, best time and cost.

## Shortlist

The working shortlist covers Pampelonne, Bouillabaisse, La Moune, Graniers, Canebiers, Salins, La Nartelle, the Sainte-Maxime beaches, Escalet, Bonne Terrasse, Douane, La Croix-Valmer beaches and Cavalaire beaches.

## Source Status

`official_partial_verification` means some facts are confirmed by municipal or official tourism sources but not yet publication-final. `seed_needs_official_verification` means the place is useful for planning but needs stronger confirmation before recommendation.

Final publication still requires official bathing-water checks, current parking details, seasonal lifeguard/toilet/shower information and local judgement on crowding and atmosphere.

## Confirmed in This Source Round

Saint-Tropez confirms 2026 lifeguard supervision for Bouillabaisse, Canebiers and Salins. Sainte-Maxime confirms La Nartelle and its wider beach list. Ramatuelle confirms Pampelonne, Escalet, Bonne Terrasse and Douane context. La Croix-Valmer confirms its coastline, shuttle context and paid beach parking season. Cavalaire confirms beach supervision periods and Bonporteau as a small wild unsupervised cove.

## Practical Segments

- Near Saint-Tropez: Bouillabaisse, Graniers, Canebiers, Salins.
- Big serviced beach day: Pampelonne.
- North side of the gulf: La Nartelle, Croisette, Garonnette, Madrague, Éléphants, Luc Provensal, Sardinaux.
- Natural Ramatuelle: Escalet, Bonne Terrasse, Douane.
- Shuttle and nature: Débarquement, Sylvabelle, Héraclée, Gigaro.
- Rougher walking coast: Brouis, Briande, Bonporteau.

## Personal Recommendation

Pampelonne is the iconic beach day, but not automatically the easiest. For comfort, La Nartelle, Cavalaire or La Croix-Valmer can be more practical; for nature, Escalet, Gigaro and Bonporteau win when access and heat are right.
