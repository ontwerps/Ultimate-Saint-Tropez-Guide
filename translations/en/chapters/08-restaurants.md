# Restaurants

## Summary

This chapter builds a practical local restaurant selection. The guide avoids generic top lists and explains why each address fits a specific moment, budget or travel day.

## Criteria

Cuisine, price level, reservations, parking, bike access, wine, best dishes, best moment and personal score.

## Shortlist

La Vague d'Or, La Voile, La Brasserie, La Muña, La Réserve à La Plage, La Tarte Tropézienne, Sénéquier, Le Club 55 and Monsieur Dior by Mauro Colagreco.

## Source Status

Cheval Blanc confirms La Vague d'Or as Arnaud Donckele's restaurant with three MICHELIN stars, five Gault&Millau toques, a 19/20 score and summer evening opening except Wednesday. La Réserve Ramatuelle confirms La Voile, La Brasserie, La Piscine, La Muña and La Réserve à La Plage as restaurant options open to non-guests. La Tarte Tropézienne confirms current brand and boutique activity.

Before publication, hours, reservation rules, current menu prices, parking, valet and local service/atmosphere judgement must be checked.

## Practical Segments

- Major gastronomic evening: La Vague d'Or, La Voile.
- Luxury lunch or sunset: La Réserve à La Plage, La Muña, Le Club 55.
- Less formal palace moment: La Brasserie.
- Iconic Saint-Tropez stop: La Tarte Tropézienne, Sénéquier.
- New 2026 scene: Monsieur Dior by Mauro Colagreco.

## Address Profiles

La Vague d'Or is a destination evening, not a casual dinner. La Voile is the gastronomic La Réserve choice. La Brasserie is the less formal palace layer. La Muña is sunset and view. La Réserve à La Plage is Pampelonne luxury logistics. La Tarte Tropézienne is the easiest iconic Saint-Tropez stop. Sénéquier is harbour theatre more than quiet value. Le Club 55 belongs to the Pampelonne myth but needs primary confirmation. Monsieur Dior is the new 2026 scene and needs current experience checks.

## Booking Strategy

Book gastronomic evenings and Pampelonne lunches early. Ask about arrival, valet, dress code, cancellation and child-friendliness. In Saint-Tropez, the return plan is part of the reservation.

## Personal Recommendation

Book La Vague d'Or or La Voile only if the evening itself is the destination. Use La Tarte Tropézienne as an easier Saint-Tropez stop; treat Sénéquier and Monsieur Dior as scene addresses until current experience, price and service are judged.
